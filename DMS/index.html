Welcome to Dunder Mifflin Scranton

Situated in the heart of Pennsylvania's electric city, our branch of the Dunder Mifflin Paper Company has been serving the Northeast with the finest paper products since 1949.

Scranton's Finest Paper

Founded by Robert Dunder and Robert Mifflin, our company has a rich heritage in the world of paper. Over the years, we've weathered challenges, pranks, and some rather unique management styles.

Paper Perfection in a Digital Age

In a world that's increasingly digital, we stand by the power of paper. Our product range includes a variety of paper types, from standard office paper to specialty products for every business need. Whether it's for your office, school, or home, we've got you covered. Check out our product page to explore our extensive catalog.

Our Mission

Paper, Pranks, and People

Our mission at Dunder Mifflin Scranton goes beyond paper. We're a company that values not just our products but also the people who make it all possible. We believe in the power of teamwork, even if it sometimes involves a few too many "that's what she said" jokes.

Our Evolution

From the NYSE to the Sabre Era

We've come a long way since our early days as a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange. The Sabre era brought its own set of challenges, and we even ventured into the world of paper companies, but through it all, we've remained dedicated to providing quality paper and endless amusement. Under the leadership of David Wallace, we've continued to grow and adapt to the ever-changing paper industry.

Our Community

Serving Scranton and Beyond

We're not just a business; we're a part of the Scranton community. Our involvement in local events, fundraisers, and, of course, the annual Office Olympics, is a testament to our commitment to the people and the city we call home.
