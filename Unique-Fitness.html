Unique Fitness

Brand Designer | 2021. Designed the new brand identity and brand collaterals.

Overview

Unique Fitness provides unique and customized training solutions for amateur and professionals in a great luxury environment. The company wanted to stand out from competition by positioning itself as an authentic and cutting edge gym.

Project scope

The founder struggled himself to get into shape for a good part of his life, and understood very well how misleading the industry is, his goal is to deliver long lasting results contrary to competition which focuses mainly on short term promises like “get 6 pack abs in 30 days” or “loose 30 lbs in 30 days” etc. you get the point. The goal is to build a community and brand loyalty for life. But before that, we must create brand awareness.

The logo

I wanted to create something distinctive and memorable and start ideation on the identity, The logo needed to bold and modern and very simple to remember, It also needed to look to good on gym equipment and merch, so instead on working on a symbol i started with the initials U+F.

The logo in motion

The 3 Colors

Fresh White

The white color represents the unique and fresh programs offered by the brand. It also represents a clean and healthy way of life.

Optimistic yellow

The yellow color represents hope and excitement for a better future. It also helps the brand stand out from the competition.

Bold black

The bold black color represents the resilience of the community. Also it gives a strong contrast to white and yellow.
